# View your device's inference telemetry
1.	Download [Azure IoT Explorer](https://github.com/Azure/azure-iot-explorer/releases).
2.	Add your IoT Hub’s connection String 
    1. Go to the [AED Portal](https://ms.portal.azure.com/#home).
    1. Select **IoT Hub**.
    1. Select the IoT Hub that your device is connected to. 
    1. From the left menu, click on **Shared access policies**.
    1. Click on **iothubowner**.
    1. Click to copy **Connection string—primary key**.
    1. Paste connections string into Azure IoT Explorer
    1. Click **connect**.
    1. Ensure that the device is pointing to an object for inferencing.
    1. Select **Telemetry**.
    1. Click **Start** to view telemetry events from device.
